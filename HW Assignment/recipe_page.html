Poha

Ingredients

  • Flattened Rice
  • onions
  • spices(Mustard Seeds,turmeric powder,curry leaves)
  • vegetables(potato,green chilly)
  • lemon juice

Instructions

  1. 2 cups thick Poha (rice flakes)
  2. 1 medium Onion, finely chopped (approx. 1/3 cup)
  3. 1 small Potato, peeled and finely chopped (approx. ½ cup)
  4. 1/3 teaspoon Mustard Seeds
  5. 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  6. 8-10 Curry Leaves
  7. 1 Green Chili, finely chopped
  8. 1 pinch of Asafoetida (Hing), optional
  9. 1½ tablespoon Peanuts (ground nuts)
  10. 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  11. 1½ teaspoons Lemon Juice
  12. 1/2 tablespoon Sugar
  13. 1 tablespoon grated Coconut, optional
  14. Salt to taste
  15. 2 tablespoons Oil
  16. 1/4 cup Pomegranate Seeds, optional
  17. 2 tablespoons finely chopped Coriander Leaves
